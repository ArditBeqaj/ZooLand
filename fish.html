Clownfish

The clownfish, also known as the anemonefish, is a small, brightly colored fish found in warm ocean waters. It is famous for its symbiotic relationship with sea anemones, where it gains protection from predators while providing the anemone with food.

The White Shark

The white shark, also known as the great white shark, is one of the most formidable predators in the ocean. It can grow up to 20 feet in length and weigh over 5,000 pounds. Known for its powerful jaws and sharp teeth, the white shark primarily feeds on seals, sea lions, and other marine animals.

Hammer Head Shark

The hammerhead shark is a distinctive species known for its unique, hammer-shaped head. Found in warm, tropical waters, this unusual head shape enhances its ability to locate prey. Hammerhead sharks can grow up to 20 feet in length and often hunt in schools during the day. They primarily feed on fish, octopuses, and crustaceans.
